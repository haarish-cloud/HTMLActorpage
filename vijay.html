Vijay

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, popularly known as Vijay, is a prominent Indian actor, playback singer, dancer, philanthropist, and politician, primarily known for his work in Tamil cinema. Early Life and Background Birth: Vijay was born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He is the son of film director S. A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar. He has a younger sister, Vidhya, who passed away at a young age. 2 Education: He attended Fathima Matriculation Higher Secondary School and later Balalok Matriculation Higher Secondary School. Vijay pursued a degree in visual communication at Loyola College but dropped out to focus on his acting career. 2 3 Sources Career in Film Debut: Vijay began his acting career as a child artist in the film "Vetri" (1984) and made his lead debut in "Naalaiya Theerpu" (1992) at the age of 18. 2 Rise to Fame: He gained popularity with films like "Poove Unakkaga" (1996) and "Kadhalukku Mariyadhai" (1997). His career took off with blockbuster hits such as "Ghilli" (2004), "Pokkiri" (2007), "Thuppakki" (2012), "Mersal" (2017), and "Master" (2021). 2 Awards and Recognition: Vijay is one of the highest-paid actors in India and has received numerous awards, including the Kalaimaamani award from the Government of Tamil Nadu. He is affectionately referred to as "Thalapathy" (Commander) by his fans. 2 2 Sources Personal Life Marriage and Family: Vijay married Sangeetha Sornalingam on August 25, 1999. The couple has two children: a son named Jason Sanjay and a daughter named Divya Saasha. 2 Philanthropy: He is known for his philanthropic efforts through his organization, Vijay Makkal Iyakkam, which focuses on community service and disaster relief. 1 3 Sources Political Career Entry into Politics: In February 2024, Vijay announced his entry into politics by launching his political party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK). His party aims to contest the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly elections. 2 2 Sources Recent Developments Current Status: As of 2024, Vijay continues to be a significant figure in both the film industry and politics, with a massive fan following not just in India but also internationally. 2 Vijay's journey from a child actor to a leading star in Tamil cinema and now a politician reflects his versatility and influence in Indian culture. His contributions to film and society have made him a beloved figure among fans and the general public alike.
